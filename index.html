We only list an initiative if it meets at least one of these criteria:

  • Registered organization with a public track record (charity ratings, government accreditation, or IFRC/UN affiliation)
  • Verified in press by a named journalist or publication — not just social media
  • Named contact person or founder with a verifiable logistics chain to Venezuela

Green ✓ = confirmed. Amber ⚠ = unconfirmed or partial. We show what we know and flag what we don’t — the reader decides.

How we find and verify

AI agents search continuously for new campaigns, press mentions, and updates across news sources in multiple languages. Every entry is then manually verified by a human before publication — no AI output goes live unreviewed. Physical collection points change rapidly; always confirm before going.

What we don’t cover

Local Venezuelan organizations: Venezuela’s 2024 Anti-NGO Law requires state authorization for civil society organizations. Hundreds were shut down or forced underground. We cannot verify independence or safety of funds routed through state-adjacent entities.
